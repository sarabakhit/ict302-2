## Why is parking such a big issue at UKY?

Parking lots often fill up quickly, especially during peak class and work hours. Students and staff end up spending extra time driving around looking for open spots, which leads to stress, lateness, and even safety concerns at night.

## How will you pay for the coverage of a Parking Pass?

We have Grants provided by the government to ensure and give our applicants if they are selected. If they qualify for our Grant then they will have a Parking Pass for that semsester. It renews every semester.

Our team recommends after the middle of the semester! Make sure to renew as soon as possible so we can get back to you quicker!
